MTD ITSA Pro

HMRC compliance built in — not chased at year end.

Making Tax Digital for Income Tax Self Assessment is mandatory from April 2026 for self-employed people earning over £50,000 — and from April 2027 for those over £30,000. If you're a cleaning business owner in England, Scotland, Wales or Northern Ireland, this applies to you. Cadi handles it entirely: your records are already digital, and quarterly submissions take a few taps.

✓Every invoice and expense logged in Cadi counts as your digital record — no extra work
✓Quarterly summaries prepared automatically in the format HMRC requires
✓Authorise once via HMRC's own portal — then submit directly from Cadi
✓Deadline alerts 2 weeks before each quarterly period closes
✓End-of-year final declaration built from your four quarterly updates

VAT threshold monitor Pro

Know before HMRC does. Not the morning after.

The UK VAT registration threshold is £90,000 of taxable turnover in any rolling 12-month period. Miss it and you're legally liable from the day you crossed it — not from when you registered. Exterior cleaning businesses are especially exposed: a busy round of 80–100 properties can tip £90k faster than expected when gutters, fascia and jet washing are added in.

✓Rolling 12-month taxable turnover tracked in real time — not calendar year, not tax year
✓Three-stage alerts: 80% (£72k), 90% (£81k), 97% (£87k)
✓Projected crossing date based on your current growth trajectory
✓Applies to residential, commercial and exterior turnover combined
