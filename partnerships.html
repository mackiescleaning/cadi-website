Common questions

Partner programme FAQs

How much commission do Affiliate Partners earn?
Affiliate Partners earn a recurring monthly commission for every active Cadi subscriber they refer. Exact rates are confirmed during onboarding — they're designed to reward long-term relationships, not just one-off sign-ups.
Does my cleaning business need to be on Cadi to become an Affiliate Partner?
Yes — we ask that Affiliate Partners are Cadi users themselves. It keeps recommendations genuine and means you'll always have first-hand answers when someone asks about the product.
Can an accountant partner access their client's Cadi account?
Accountant Partners get a read-only view via the accountant portal — you can see submission status, income summaries and upcoming obligations without having the client's login. Full access would require explicit permission from the client.
Can I be both an Affiliate Partner and a Trade Partner?
In most cases, yes. If you run a cleaning business and also supply cleaning products, both programmes can apply simultaneously. We'll confirm the arrangement during onboarding.
How long does the application process take?
We aim to respond to every application within 5 working days. Applications that include more detail about your audience, client base or business will be reviewed faster.
Is there a minimum audience size for Creator Partners?
We don't set a hard minimum — a tight 500-person Facebook group of active cleaning business owners is more valuable to us than a 50,000-subscriber general business channel. Tell us about your audience and we'll assess fit.
What sectors are Trade Partnerships open to?
Cleaning chemicals and consumables, equipment manufacturers, van and vehicle suppliers, insurance providers, training course operators, trade associations (BICSc, NCCA, etc.) and any business whose primary customer base is cleaning business owners.
When do payouts happen for commission-based partners?
Commission is calculated at the end of each calendar month and paid within 14 days. A minimum payout threshold applies — details confirmed during onboarding.
