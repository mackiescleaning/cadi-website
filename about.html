Built from the van up

We didn't spot a gap
in the market.
We lived the problem.

Cadi was built by two cleaning business owners who got fed up looking for software that understood them. So they stopped looking and started building.

The origin story
"Chris and I run separate sides of Mackies Cleaning — interior and exterior. We couldn't find a single piece of software that worked for both. So we built our own."

It started with frustration. Not the dramatic kind — just the slow, grinding frustration of jumping between four different apps to get through a single day. Scheduling in one place, invoicing in another, accounts somewhere else entirely, and staff management held together with WhatsApp messages and a spreadsheet that nobody trusted.

We tried everything. ZenMaid. CleanerPlanner. Xero. Jobber. Good tools — but none of them were built for a business like ours. Interior and exterior cleaning under one roof. A growing team. Real ambition. Every tool we tried made us fit around it. None of them fitted around us.

"We were spending money as fast as it came in. Not really knowing what we'd made until the end of the year. Wondering if we could even afford to hire — or if bringing someone on would sink us."

That fear of growth. The paralysis of not being able to see your own numbers clearly enough to make a call. That's an awful place to run a business from. And we knew we weren't alone — every cleaning business owner we spoke to felt the same way.

So we built Cadi. Not as a side project. Not as a pivot. As a genuine answer to a genuine problem — one we were living every single week. Pricing calculators that are actually accurate for cleaning. Scheduling that handles both interior rounds and exterior jobs. Accounting connected directly to HMRC. A staff portal that shows you the true cost of a hire before you commit. A business health score that tells you not just what's happening — but what to do about it.

Features no other cleaning software has even thought of. Because no other cleaning software was built by people still in the van.

Take the pricing calculator. Chris spent 17+ years collecting real customer data from Mackies Cleaning — not just what jobs cost, but what happens after. Which prices led to loyal, long-term customers. Which ones attracted one-off jobs that went nowhere. The calculator isn't just about winning the job. It's about winning the right job. Lifetime customer value, baked into the price from day one. No other software in the cleaning industry has ever done that.
